-ABOUT ME-

Hi! My name is Thomas. I am a student originally from Australia, currently studying

in Germany. I have a deep passion for Computer Science, particularly in the

development and implementation of projects. Seeing my work come to fruition is

what drives me to pursue this career. I am hardworking and take great pride in

spearheading projects, especially when I am deeply interested in the topic.

EDUCATION

I am currently studying information engineering at the Technische Universität München.

This Bachelor focuses on the theoretical aspect of Computer Science with courses

complementing the theoretical side through the application of the given theory (primarily

through the use of JAVA). In addition to the theoretical and practical courses we also

have several courses which focus on the business side of developing and implementing a

digital system. This combination has enabled me to learn the full lifecycle of an

information system, from the planning, development, implementation and then finally the

maintenance involve with information system.
